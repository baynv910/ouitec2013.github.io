ABOUT US

--------

OU-IT’s English Club, directly under Faculty of Information Technology. Established on Aug 10th, 2013, running by students with highly support from Lectures. With our slogan “Lead your passion”, we’re always try our best to inspire students to learn English more effectively. ITEC’s activities are intended to create an active environment for all English lovers, through those things with us, you may improve yourself in both soft-skill and English-skill which are such as teamwork, presenting, critical thinking, … or speaking and listening skill (in English) – those are not offer much in our lesson in the school! Moreover, how can it be when you’re an ITEC member? Like a home! You may practise more with us, focus on soft-skill and training your attitude, those are what the employee want to see from you, more than just knowledge!
So, what did, what do, and what will we do? If you want to try a real TOEIC test but with just 1$ for fee, come to our TOEIC Mock Test. If you want to have an English environment or just a partner to practise speaking and listening English, come to our Speaking English Activities. There are lots of documents to learn English on the Internet but you don’t have able to find out which one is suit for you, or just like you don’t confide in those things, don’t be worry, we will offer for you on our Fanpage! Our secret activities are waiting for you to take an adventure! And now, follow us, and enjoy undergraduate time with us!
